My Photography Philosophy

When my children were young, I would get frustrated at how expensive it was to get photos taken of my family. I couldn't afford to pay the "sitting fee," let alone the high prices they wanted for each print that I wanted to order. I finally just started taking my own pictures and learned how to edit them until I got a photo that I absolutely loved. I took those skills and my love of photography to my friends and family by shooting special events for them. I would now like to share those skills with you. I would like to give you what I wanted when my children were younger... tons of pictures at affordable prices.
